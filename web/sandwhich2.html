Veg Grilled Sandwich

Ingredients of Homemade Veg Grilled Sandwich

• 1 onion
• 1 boiled potato
• 1/2 capsicum
• 1 small cucumber
• 1 carrot, grated
• 100 gram paneer, grated
• 4 cheese slices
• 4 tbsp mayonnaise
• to taste Salt
• to taste Black pepper powder
• as required Tomato sauce

How to Make Homemade Veg Grilled Sandwich

1. Cut cucumber, onion and capsicum into this slices.
2. Mix grated carrot and paneer. Add in mayonnaise.
3. Smear 4 bread slices with some tomato sauce. Sprinkle salt and pepper.
4. Place the veggies on all of them.
5. Top it with grated veggie mix.
6. Place cheese slice. Cover with another slice and grill with butter.
